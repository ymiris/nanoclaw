## Professional Summary
2-3 sentence summary tailored to target role emphasizing:
- Years of leadership experience in support/customer success
- Revenue impact and team scale
- Relevant technical expertise for the specific role

Example:
"Director-level support leader with 17+ years driving global customer success operations for enterprise SaaS platforms. Built monetized support model generating $1.8M ARR while scaling teams across NA, EMEA, and APAC. Expert in Zendesk, Salesforce Service Cloud, and AI-powered support transformation."

## Customization Guidelines

### For Director-Level Roles
- Lead with monetization achievement ($1.8M ARR)
- Emphasize strategic planning and executive stakeholder management
- Highlight cross-functional influence (Sales, Product, Engineering)

### For Senior Manager Roles
- Lead with team scale and operational excellence
- Emphasize metrics transformation (CSAT, resolution time)
- Highlight hands-on technical expertise

### For AI/Data Company Roles
- Emphasize Domino Data Lab context (enterprise data science platform)
- Highlight AI implementation in support operations
- Feature technical depth with Salesforce/Zendesk

### For SaaS/Enterprise Software Roles
- Lead with Fortune 500 customer experience
- Emphasize 24/7/365 global operations
- Highlight support-as-revenue-driver model
