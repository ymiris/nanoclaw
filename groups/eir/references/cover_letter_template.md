# Cover Letter Template

## Structure

### Opening Paragraph
Hook with specific connection to company/role:

**Template Options:**

**For growth-stage companies:**
"I'm writing to express my strong interest in the [Job Title] position at [Company]. Having built a monetized support organization from the ground up at Domino Data Lab—generating $1.8M ARR while maintaining 98% CSAT—I'm drawn to [Company's] mission of [specific company goal/mission] and the opportunity to scale customer success operations during this critical growth phase."

**For enterprise-focused roles:**
"As a director-level support leader with 17+ years serving Fortune 500 customers, I was immediately drawn to the [Job Title] opportunity at [Company]. My track record of building global support operations across NA, EMEA, and APAC—while reducing resolution times from 30 to 12 days—aligns directly with [Company's] commitment to enterprise customer excellence."

**For AI/tech-forward companies:**
"The [Job Title] role at [Company] represents the perfect intersection of my passion for AI-powered customer success and my proven track record scaling support operations. At Domino Data Lab, I've led support for enterprise data science platforms while implementing [specific AI/automation initiative], generating measurable business impact through [specific metric]."

### Body Paragraph 1: Demonstrate Strategic Impact
Connect a major achievement to their specific need:

**Template:**
"At Domino Data Lab, I identified an opportunity to transform support from a cost center to a revenue driver. Through competitive analysis of Databricks, AWS, and leading SaaS platforms, I designed a three-tier monetized support model that generated $1.8M ARR. This experience directly aligns with [Company's] need for [specific requirement from JD], demonstrating my ability to [specific capability they need]."

**Variations based on JD emphasis:**
- If emphasizing team building: Focus on scaling from X to 11 direct reports
- If emphasizing metrics: Lead with CSAT improvement 72% → 98%
- If emphasizing global ops: Focus on 24/7/365 across three regions
- If emphasizing customer-facing: Highlight Fortune 500 experience

### Body Paragraph 2: Address Specific Requirements
Pick 2-3 key requirements from JD and map to experience:

**Template:**
"Your requirement for [Requirement 1] resonates with my experience [specific example with metric]. Additionally, [Requirement 2] aligns with [another specific achievement]. I'm particularly excited about [Requirement 3/challenge mentioned in JD] as I've successfully [relevant experience]."

### Body Paragraph 3: Cultural/Strategic Fit
Show understanding of company stage/challenges:

**For high-growth companies:**
"I'm energized by [Company's] growth trajectory and understand the unique challenges of scaling support while maintaining quality. My experience building documentation hubs (support.domino.ai), implementing tiered support models, and establishing cross-functional collaboration with Sales and Product would enable immediate impact."

**For established enterprises:**
"[Company's] position as [industry description] requires a support leader who can balance operational excellence with strategic innovation. My background serving Fortune 500 customers in enterprise software, combined with my technical foundation (BS Computer Science, Salesforce Admin certification), positions me to drive both day-to-day operations and long-term strategic initiatives."

**For AI/technical companies:**
"What excites me about [Company] is [specific technology/mission]. Having led support operations for enterprise data science platforms, I understand the unique technical complexity and customer education challenges in [their domain]. My hands-on experience with [relevant tech stack] and commitment to continuous learning would enable me to advocate effectively for both customers and internal teams."

### Closing Paragraph
**Template:**
"I'd welcome the opportunity to discuss how my experience building revenue-generating support operations, leading global teams, and driving measurable customer success outcomes can contribute to [Company's] continued growth. Thank you for considering my application."

**Sign-off:**
```
Best regards,
[Name]
```

---

## Customization Guidelines

### Length
Target 3/4 page to 1 full page maximum. Hiring managers are busy.

### Tone Calibration
- **Startup/high-growth**: Energetic, action-oriented, emphasize speed and impact
- **Enterprise**: Professional, measured, emphasize stability and scalability
- **Technical**: Include more technical terminology, emphasize systems thinking

### What NOT to Include
- Don't rehash entire resume
- Don't use generic phrases like "I'm a team player"
- Don't mention salary expectations (unless explicitly requested)
- Don't explain gaps or weaknesses (address if asked in interview)

### Personalization Musts
- Reference specific company initiatives, products, or news
- Connect to their stated challenges/goals in JD
- Show you understand their market position
- Demonstrate knowledge of their tech stack if mentioned
